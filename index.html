How to use:
  • Download your cube in SVG format from OpenSea (should be in this format by default)
  • Click the "Browse" button above
  • Select your downloaded cube and watch it animate with the preset color and animation options
  • You can customize the default colors by clicking "Toggle color options" below. The format is an array of [r,g,b] number value arrays or hex codes wrapped in quotes (eg - like using your own colors from your OpenPalette tokens)
